Weclome to Dunder Mifflin's Purchase Request System!


With PRS, employees can request items for themselves or their departments for work purposes.

Use the menu at the top of the page to navigate around the site.

If you need guidance on where to start, try searching through requests here to see if you've already created what you're looking for. If you are looking to update or change a request, find your request on the list and follow the buttons and link to make the changes you'd like to make.

If you need to make a new request, click here.

If you're correctly logged in, your username will appear at the top right of the screen. If you need to approve or reject a request, you will not be able to view your own submissions.

For more information, visit the help or about pages.
